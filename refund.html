Refund & Return Policy

Aa’In Chocolates – Refund & Return Policy

Effective Date: September 10, 2025

Scope of Refunds/Returns

Due to perishability, returns are only accepted if:

  • The product arrives defective, melted, or incorrect.
  • The product is past its Best Before Date at delivery.

Time Frame

Report issues within 24 hours of delivery or collection.

Evidence Required

Submit order number, a description of the issue, and clear photos when contacting us.

Resolutions Offered

Depending on the issue, we’ll provide a replacement or refund (to the original payment method) within 7–14 business days.

Non-Refundable Conditions

  • Customized or trademark-removed orders are not eligible for refund or cancellation once production starts.
  • Claims made after 24 hours are generally not accepted, unless exceptional circumstances are evidenced.

Cancellations

Standard orders can be canceled within 12 hours if production hasn’t begun.

No cancellations for customized or bulk orders after confirmation.
